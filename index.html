How it works

Five steps from idea to approval

1

Free consultation

We visit your home, discuss your ideas and assess feasibility — including your permitted development rights — at no cost.

2

Measured survey

Our surveyor carries out a full measured survey of the property, typically within 7–10 working days of instruction.

3

Design & drawings

We produce existing and proposed plans using the latest CAD software, with draft options to help you finalise the design.

4

Planning submission

We submit electronically and manage the entire process with the local authority, keeping you fully informed throughout.

5

Building control

Once approved, we prepare detailed building regulation drawings so your contractor can build with confidence.
