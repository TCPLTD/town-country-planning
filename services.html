What we offer

Our services

From a single-storey kitchen extension to a complex planning appeal, we provide a complete architectural and planning service tailored to your project.

01
Residential

Extensions & loft conversions

Get a quote ›

The most popular way to add space and value to your home. Whether you are looking for a larger kitchen, an extra bedroom, a home office or simply more living space, we can design an extension that makes the very best of your property and plot.

Many extensions can be built under Permitted Development rights, meaning no formal planning application is required. We will advise you on this at the outset — often saving both time and cost. Where planning permission is needed, we manage the entire process on your behalf.

Extension types we design

  • Single-storey rear extensions
  • Double-storey rear extensions
  • Side return extensions
  • Wrap-around extensions
  • Over-garage extensions
  • Front porch extensions

Loft conversions

  • Dormer loft conversions
  • Hip-to-gable conversions
  • Mansard conversions
  • Velux / rooflight conversions
  • L-shaped dormers
  • Full loft redesigns
02
Development

New build dwellings

Get a quote ›

For projects requiring the demolition of an existing dwelling or the development of a new residential building, we provide full design and planning consultancy services from initial concept through to planning consent.

We have extensive experience of new build residential projects across North London and Hertfordshire, including replacement dwellings on existing residential plots, new dwellings in rear gardens, and small residential developments.

We also handle complex planning appeal submissions to the Planning Inspectorate where initial applications have been refused. Our track record of appeal successes reflects our thorough understanding of planning policy and inspector reasoning.

New build services include

  • Replacement dwellings
  • New build on residential plots
  • Pre-application advice
  • Planning statements
  • Design & access statements
  • Planning appeal submissions
  • Liaison with planning officers
  • Condition discharge
03
Consultancy

Planning consultancy

Get a quote ›

Not every project requires full architectural drawings from the outset. Sometimes you simply need expert advice before committing to a course of action. We offer standalone planning consultancy services to homeowners and developers who need professional guidance on the planning process.

We can advise on the likelihood of planning approval for a proposed scheme, advise on design changes that would improve prospects of consent, assess permitted development rights, and prepare the supporting documents required to accompany a planning application.

Consultancy services

  • Pre-application advice
  • Permitted development assessment
  • Planning statements
  • Design & access statements
  • Householder statements
  • Planning appeal support
  • LPA pre-application submissions
  • Commercial development advice
04
Conversions

Garage & flat conversions

Get a quote ›

Converting an existing structure is often the most cost-effective way to add usable space to your home. Garage conversions in particular can be carried out under Permitted Development in many cases, and we will advise you on this at the outset.

For flat conversions and sub-divisions, we have significant experience of preparing planning applications for houses in multiple occupation (HMOs) and conversions from single dwellings to flats across Barnet, Enfield and neighbouring boroughs.

Conversion types

  • Integral garage conversions
  • Detached garage conversions
  • Basement conversions
  • House to flat conversion
  • Commercial to residential
  • Change of use applications
05
Outbuildings

Garden offices & outbuildings

Get a quote ›

The demand for high-quality garden offices and outbuildings has grown significantly in recent years. Many can be built without planning permission under Permitted Development rights, subject to size and siting constraints. We will assess your specific property and advise accordingly.

Where planning permission is required — for example in Conservation Areas or on listed properties — we will prepare and manage the full application on your behalf.

Outbuilding types

  • Garden offices
  • Home studios
  • Annexes and ancillary rooms
  • Swimming pool buildings
  • Garden stores and workshops
  • Summer houses
06
Technical

Building control drawings

Get a quote ›

Once planning permission has been granted, building regulations approval is the next essential step. We prepare detailed technical drawings and specifications for submission to an independent Building Control body, giving your builders everything they need to price and carry out the work correctly.

Where structural calculations are required, we can coordinate with our network of structural engineers to ensure the submission is complete and accurate. All our building control drawings are produced to the same high standard as our planning drawings.

Building control services include

  • Full building regulation drawings
  • Specification of materials
  • Structural co-ordination
  • Submission to Building Control
  • Response to queries
  • Drainage design
