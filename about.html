About us

A practice built on trust

Town and Country Planning Ltd has been helping homeowners and developers across North London and Hertfordshire navigate the planning process since 2009.

Who we are

Town and Country Planning Ltd is an independent architectural planning consultancy based in Cockfosters, Barnet. Founded by Martin McGahon, the practice has grown through word-of-mouth recommendation to become one of the most trusted planning consultancies in North London and Hertfordshire.

We work primarily with homeowners looking to extend, convert or rebuild their homes, as well as developers and commercial clients with more complex requirements. Whatever the scale of your project, you receive the same dedicated personal service.

Our strength lies in a combination of creative design, thorough knowledge of local planning policy, and longstanding working relationships with local authorities across the region. We know what works, and we know how to guide a proposal through the planning process efficiently.

All our drawing work is produced using the latest CAD technology, providing you with drawings that will be clearly understood by your builders, planners and building control officers.

We manage the entire process with local authorities and keep our clients fully informed of progress at every stage. We also maintain a network of reliable building contractors and structural engineers we can recommend when the time comes to build.

Our approach

Bespoke personal service
Every client and every project is different. We take time to understand your needs before we draw a single line.
Expert local knowledge
We have in-depth knowledge of planning policy across Barnet, Enfield, Hertsmere and neighbouring authorities.
End-to-end management

High success rate
Our track record of planning approvals reflects both our design quality and our thorough understanding of what local authorities will accept.
